Phono-semantic compound (形聲, OC *laːɡ): semantic 言 (“say”) + phonetic 睪 (OC *neb, *laːɡ, *kuː).

Etymology[edit]

Same word as 繹 (OC *laːɡ, “to unravel”) (Schuessler, 2007). See there for more.

Definitions[edit]

譯

  1. to translate; to interpret
    直譯／直译  ―  zhíyì  ―  to translate literally
    把書譯成中文／把书译成中文  ―  bǎ shū yì chéng zhōngwén  ―  to translate the book into Chinese
  2. to decode; to decipher
  3. a surname

Synonyms[edit]

  • (to translate): 翻譯／翻译 (fānyì), 傳譯／传译 (chuányì), 翻 (fān)

Hyponyms[edit]

  • (to translate): 筆譯／笔译 (bǐyì, “to do written translation”), 口譯／口译 (kǒuyì, “to do interpreting”)
