    Definitions[edit]

    湖

    1. lake; loch
    2. Short for 湖北 (Húběi, “Hubei Province”).
    3. Short for 湖南 (Húnán, “Hunan Province”).

    Synonyms[edit]

    • (lake):

    • 泊 (pō) (literary, or in compounds)
    • 海子 (hǎizi) (northern and southwestern Mandarin, chiefly in toponyms)
    • 湖泊 (húpō)
